The best beaches in Venezuela

Cayo de agua, Los Roques

Cayo de Agua is considered one of the most beautiful beaches in the country. It is located in the Los Roques Archipelago, and is the furthest key from tourists. It has the particularity that with a natural bridge it joins two very close islands and according to the conditions of the tide the bridge can be appreciated or you will see how the marine currents that collide with each other are confused. One of the most practiced activities in this place is diving since the sea is crystal clear and you will be able to observe a lot of fish and corals. Don't forget to bring sunscreen!.

NORONKY BEACH

This remote island beach off the coast of Venezuela is quite wild so nature shows itself in all its splendor. It is ideal for contemplating marine fauna without the need for sophisticated diving equipment. You will be able to see corals and starfish with snorkel masks. If you are lucky you can even see sea turtles in the area.

BIG BEACH or CHORONI

This tropical paradise is one of the best beaches in Venezuela. The beach has golden sand and blue waters that are framed by the lush greenery of the park. It is also one of the favorite destinations for Venezuelan surfers due to the strong waves that the sea has in this area.
